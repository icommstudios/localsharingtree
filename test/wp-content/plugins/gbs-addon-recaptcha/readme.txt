GBS Captcha
===========

Adds a Captcha (using reCaptcha) to the Registration page.

Installation
------------
1. Signup for reCaptcha at http://www.google.com/recaptcha/whyrecaptcha
2. Copy the public and private key for configuration in step 5.
3. Upload and activate plugin.
4. Go to Group Buying > General Options, scroll to bottom of page and check the box for the "Registration Captcha" module you want to activate.
5. New settings will be available on the same general options page (above the registration options section), paste your public and private keys and save.
6. Recaptcha will automatically be added to the registration page.
