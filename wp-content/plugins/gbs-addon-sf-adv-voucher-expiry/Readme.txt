Advanced Voucher Expiry Add-on for GBS (GroupBuyingSite.com)
Version: 1.3
Platform: Wordpress & GroupBuyingSite.com
Platform Version: Wordpress 3 & GBS 3, GBS 4
Author: Daniel Schuring / StudioFidelis.com
Author Email: contact@studiofidelis.com
=========================================================

Description: 
---------------------------------------------------------
Adds an option to set the number of days a Voucher expires after the date of Purchase.

Use this it create items that have a limited amount of days to be redeemed, such as a Ticket to an event, or a limited time discount on products or services.  


Features: 
---------------------------------------------------------
1) Turn on/off Voucher expiry per Deal (Default is off)

2) If a Voucher has expired, access to download or print the Vouchers will be blocked.
